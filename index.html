About the Company

The Quiet North LLC is a U.S.-registered publishing company dedicated to educational and cognitive wellness books. We serve a broad audience — from children to older adults — with high-quality content supported by professionals in education, psychology, and design.

Our books are distributed internationally through major platforms and combine the benefits of print and digital formats.

  • U.S.-registered LLC
  • Print & digital publishing
  • Professionally developed content
  • International distribution

Publishing Focus

  • Cognitive Wellness: Memory and focus training workbooks for adults and seniors.
  • Educational Puzzle Books: Engaging activities for children aged 4–14 that develop logic and creativity.
  • Intergenerational Trivia: Books that connect families through shared memories and spark conversations.
  • Hybrid Print + Digital: Selected books include online content and printable resources via QR codes.

Our Values

We believe in intentional publishing. Our mission is to publish books that matter—rooted in science, clarity, and emotional impact. We focus on accessibility and long-term value over trends or volume. Our long-term vision is to become a reference in educational and therapeutic publishing, delivering accessible content in both print and digital formats.
