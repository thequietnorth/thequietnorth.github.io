About the Company

The Quiet North LLC is a U.S.-registered publishing company dedicated to developing and distributing books that foster cognitive wellness, education, and creativity. We produce high-quality print and digital titles for a global audience—from children to older adults—designed by professionals and backed by thoughtful editorial standards.

What We Do

  • Cognitive Wellness: Structured workbooks and memory training programs for adults and seniors.
  • Educational Puzzles: Engaging, age-appropriate activity books for children aged 4 to 14.
  • Intergenerational Games: Nostalgic trivia and logic activities that connect families.
  • Hybrid Print + Digital: Books with optional online content to extend the reader’s experience.
