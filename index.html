About the Company

The Quiet North LLC is a U.S.-registered publishing company dedicated to educational and cognitive wellness books. We serve a broad audience — from children to older adults — with high-quality content supported by professionals in education, psychology, and design.

Our books are distributed internationally through major platforms and combine the benefits of print and digital formats.

  • U.S.-registered LLC
  • Print & digital publishing
  • Professionally developed content
  • International distribution

Publishing Focus

  • Cognitive Wellness: Workbooks and memory challenges for seniors and adults.
  • Educational Puzzle Books: Themed content that develops logic and creativity in children aged 4–14.
  • Trivia & Family Games: Intergenerational books that spark conversations and memories.
  • Digital Integration: Select books include QR codes for online extras and printable resources.

Our Values

We believe in intentional publishing. Our mission is to produce books that matter—rooted in science, clarity, and care. We prioritize emotional impact, accessibility, and long-term value over trends or volume.
