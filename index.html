About The Quiet North

The Quiet North LLC is a U.S.-registered publishing company focused on the development and global distribution of books with educational and wellness value. Our mission is to create printed and digital products that inspire personal growth, support mental well-being, and encourage lifelong learning.

We serve a wide audience — ranging from children to older adults — by offering high-quality books designed with care, research, and creativity.

Our Publishing Focus

  • Cognitive Wellness and Brain Training: Structured workbooks and activities supporting memory, concentration, and mental clarity in adults and seniors.
  • Children’s Educational Books: Engaging puzzle books and logic-based challenges designed for young readers aged 4 to 14.
  • Intergenerational Games: Nostalgic trivia and reflective challenges that promote meaningful connection across age groups.
  • Hybrid Print + Digital Experiences: Optional online extras via QR codes, enhancing engagement with printable resources and guided content.
